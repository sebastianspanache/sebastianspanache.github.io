SEBASTIAN SPANACHE

UPAMERCADO

Released October 14th 2023

The solo album is a culmination of my travels and experiences in Latin America from 2017 to 2020. Recorded in mono. Listen on Bandcamp

Produced by Jazz Updates;
Recorded at the Faculty of Theater and Music of West University, Timișoara;
Financed through an Energie! Creative Grant of the National Cultural Program 'Timișoara - European Capital of Culture in 2023', Romania;
Composed by Sebastian Spanache.

I've transcribed ULTRA DETAILED piano sheet music of all six tracks in PDF format. Get them on Gumroad



ALONE TOGETHER

Released May 15th 2021

Solo improvised live performance part of 'Chamber Jazz @ UT' invitational concerts conceived during the COVID-19 pandemic.

The series featured six of the most representative Romanian jazz pianists and improvisers offering their take on the classic jazz standard Alone Together. Watch on YouTube

Filmed at the Multicultural Center of Transilvania University,
Brașov, Romania.
